MY STORY

There are a lot of great technologies out there that can aid you in your video production, and the following article will tell you how to use them. The key to modern video production is not only the quality of equipment that you’re using but also how well it interacts with other pieces of technology, such as Shotoku’s VICON system. When you purchase your Shotoku system, it comes with a high level of compatibility and interconnection. This makes it easy to connect your Shotoku system to other devices and upgrades as they become available. If you’re going to be using the Shotoku system for professional video production, you’ll want to snag their VICON Fast Lens Adapter, which will work well for most fast lenses. It’s the most hassle-free way of getting a lens on the system without having to fuss with adapters or screws. You can attach a wide range of lenses onto this adapter and achieve high quality video even when zooming in on distant objects.
